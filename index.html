About Sara

Healing Through Understanding

I'm Sara Adham, the owner and founder of Nafsi Clinic. As a CBT therapist with experience from Cady Academy, I offer online mental health sessions in Arabic, English, and Swedish.

I have authored books on narcissism and personal growth, and created a complete CBT certification programme through Nafsi Academy.

عن سارة

الشفاء من خلال الفهم

أنا سارة أدهم، مؤسسة عيادة نفسي. أنا معالجة متخصصة في العلاج المعرفي السلوكي (CBT) بخبرة من أكاديمية كادي، أقدم جلسات عبر الإنترنت بالعربية والإنجليزية والسويدية.

Programme Overview

What This Covers

Sara Adham's flagship certification — a complete two-level CBT programme from foundational theory to advanced clinical application.

CBT Level 1 — Foundations

Core cognitive and behavioural principles, thought pattern identification, emotional regulation, and foundational CBT tools.

CBT Level 2 — Advanced Practice

Complex case conceptualisation, advanced CBT techniques, schema therapy integration, and real-world transformation.
